Driver License Suspension Notifications To Your Phone.

We'll check your driver license monthly for free; no credit card required.
Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach and Orange County.

How it works

Subscribe to Drive Fine, by completing the form. Monthly we'll check your driver license and If there is a change to you driver license status like a suspension, we'll send you a notification to your phone number and/or email used to subscribe with additional information.

The Impact

Almost 2 Million Floridians have their license suspended.[1] In 2017, over 1.1 million suspension notices, making up nearly 72% of all suspension notices that year, were for unpaid fines and fees, not for dangerous driving or public safety concerns. [2]

Our Mission

Our mission is to help users stay informed of their driving record with proactive updates and reports.

User data

We take our users data security very seriously and use encryption of data and communication. You can stop reciving SMS notifications by replying stop to your last notification, for emails you can unsubscribe.

Cost

Currently this service is free to use with no credit card required and limited to one driver's license per person. If you would like to donate or need to subscribe to multiple driver licenses please email support@drivefine.com
